Directions

This simulation demonstrates parallel modes of heat transfer from a cylindrical reactor vessel to the surroundings. Use the tabs to switch between cases featuring radiation-only heat loss, combined radiation and convection losses, and a safety design challenge. Adjust the sliders to see how different parameters affect the results, and hover over the schematics to see temperature profiles across the reactor wall.
