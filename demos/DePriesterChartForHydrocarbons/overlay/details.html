Here is an example of how to use a DePriester chart for propane at 70 psia and 20 °F:

  1. Use the sliders in controls panel to set the pressure to 70 psia and temperature to 20 °F.
  2. The green line will automatically connect these two points on the chart.
  3. The K-value is read where that green line intersects the propane curve (K = 0.8 in this example).

You can experiment with other pressure/temperature combinations to see how K changes.
