Directions

Learning objective:
Understand how divergence of velocity is related to mass balance.

How to use the simulation:
The simulation shows a vector plot of five different velocity profiles. Use the list on the left to select a profile. Move the 2 x 2 box by adjusting the x-center and y-center sliders. The numbers next to each side show the net flow rate through that face: positive indicates flow out of the box and negative indicates flow into the box.

Quantitative understanding:
Compute the divergence of each of the five profiles and verify that the inflow and outflow rates balance whenever the divergence is zero.
