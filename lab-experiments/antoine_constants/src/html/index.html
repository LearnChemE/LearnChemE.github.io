Directions

This is a digital experiment in which the user determines Antoine constants via linear or nonlinear regression. Select one of the species to inject, a temperature for the evacuated chamber to be held at, and a volume to inject. Next, fill the syringe with the selected species and inject it into the evacuated chamber. The pressure will take a few seconds to equilibrate. To add additional liquid into the chamber, you may fill the syringe again and inject multiple times. To empty the chamber and start a new measurement, press "reset". The corresponding worksheet accompanying this digital experiment can be downloaded by pressing "Worksheet".
