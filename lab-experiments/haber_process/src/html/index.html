Directions

This digital experiment measures the equilibrium compositions for ammonia formation from hydrogen and nitrogen. Equilibrium can be obtained at different pressures and temperatures and the equilibrium constant is calculated assuming ideal gases. The equilibrium constants obtained over a range of temperatures are used to calculate the heat of reaction. Use the scroll wheel to zoom.
