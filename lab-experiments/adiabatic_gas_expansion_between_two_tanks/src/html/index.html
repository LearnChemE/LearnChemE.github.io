Directions

This digital experiment expands an ideal gas, which is at high pressure in the left tank, adiabatically into the right tank, which is initially evacuated. Select the volume of each tank (25, 50 or 100 L), and set the initial pressure and temperature of the gas in the left tank with the sliders. Then open the valves on the top of the two tanks by clicking them. To expand the gas into the right tank, open the valve connecting the tanks by clicking on it. Hover your mouse over the pressure gauges to expand their size for readability. Reset the simulation by clicking the "Reset" button. Click the "Worksheet" button to download the worksheet for this experiment.
