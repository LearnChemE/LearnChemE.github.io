Directions

This simulation models an extended area (cross-flow) heat exchanger. Use the switches for the pump and fan to turn them on/off. Drag the thermometer to change what it measures, and rotate the flow dial to change the flowrate setpoint. Scroll to zoom and drag the screen to pan.
