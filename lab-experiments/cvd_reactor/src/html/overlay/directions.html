Directions

In this digital experiment, silicon dioxide is deposited on a solid surface in the reactor by chemical vapor deposition. A tetraethyl orthosilicate (TEOS)/He stream is fed to the reactor. A recycle valve allows a fraction of the effluent from the reactor to be recycled. The rate of mass gain of the solid surface is measured using a quartz crystal microbalance (QCM). See the worksheet for more details. Zoom with the scroll wheel.
